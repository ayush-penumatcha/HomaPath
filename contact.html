Frequently Asked Questions

Is HomaPath free to download?
Yes, HomaPath is completely free to download from the Google Play Store. We believe in supporting our spiritual community, so all essential ritual management features are available at no cost.
Does it work without internet?
Absolutely. HomaPath is built with 'Offline-First' technology. You can view, add, and edit bookings even without an active internet connection. The app automatically synchronizes your data with the secure cloud once you are back online.
How do I reset my password?
Security is our priority. If you forget your password, simply go to the login screen and tap the 'Forgot Password?' link. Enter your registered email address, and we will instantly send you a secure link to reset your credentials.
Is my booking data safe?
Yes, your data is extremely safe. We use enterprise-grade Google Firebase infrastructure with SSL encryption for all data transfers. Furthermore, our internal security rules ensure that only you can access and manage your specific ritual records.
Can I add custom Homa types?
While the app comes pre-loaded with over 20 traditional Homa types, we understand every region has unique rituals. You can easily add and manage your own custom Homa categories through the preferences menu in your Pandit profile.
How many bookings can I add?
There is no limit. You can add an unlimited number of bookings and maintain your entire history of rituals for years to come. HomaPath is designed to scale with your growing spiritual practice.
Does the WhatsApp feature work on all phones?
The WhatsApp integration works on any Android device that has the official WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app installed. It allows you to quickly share ritual details, location pins, and blessings with your yajamans in one tap.
