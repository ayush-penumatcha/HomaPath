Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

📜 1. Introduction

HomaPath is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while providing you with the ultimate digital companion for your sacred duties. We treat your data with the same reverence and care that we apply to our ancient Vedic traditions.

🔍 2. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless experience, we collect information that allows you to manage your rituals effectively:

  • Account Information: Your full name, email address, and encrypted password used for authentication.
  • Profile Details: Pandit title, city, temple affiliation, member since date, and profile photo.
  • Homa Booking Details: Comprehensive data about each ritual, including Homa type, yajaman (client) name, gotra, mobile number, venue, date, and time.
  • Financial Records: Advance amounts, total dakshina amounts, and payment status for each booking.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device data and notification tokens to ensure reminders are delivered correctly.
  • User Preferences: Your selected app languages (Telugu, Hindi, Sanskrit, etc.) and ritual specializations.

🛠️ 3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to enhance your ritual management workflow:

  • Ritual Management: Organising and displaying your Homa bookings in calendar and list views.
  • Divine Reminders: Sending automated push notifications to remind you of upcoming sacred ceremonies.
  • Communication: Facilitating WhatsApp integration so you can share booking details and blessings with yajamans.
  • Account Security: Verifying your identity during login and enabling password recovery via email.
  • App Improvement: Analyzing anonymized usage patterns to improve the HomaPath experience for all Pandits.

☁️ 4. Firebase & Google Services

We use Google Firebase for secure data storage and real-time synchronization. Your data is stored on highly secure Google Cloud servers with industry-standard encryption. Firebase Auth ensures that your login credentials remain private and protected at all times.

🛡️ 5. Data Security

We implement multi-layered security measures to protect your information:

  • Firestore Security Rules: Sophisticated database rules ensure that only YOU can read or write your personal ritual data. No other user can access your bookings.
  • SSL Encryption: All data transmission between your device and our servers is protected by high-grade SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Offline Protection: Local data stored on your device for offline use is protected by standard mobile operating system security.

⚖️ 6. Your Rights

You maintain full control over your sacred data:

  • Access & Export: You can view and manage all your bookings and profile data directly within the app at any time.
  • Rectification: You have the right to update or correct any information in your profile or bookings instantly.
  • Account Deletion: You can request full account and data deletion at any time by contacting us. We will ensure all your ritual records are permanently removed from our servers.
  • Transparency: You have the right to know exactly what data we store and how it is being used.
